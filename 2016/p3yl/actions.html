PROV: Three Years Later

Summary of Actions

Full IRC log is available from https://www.w3.org/2016/06/06-p3yl-irc.txt.

The attendees prioritised three key actions for the community:

  1. A community portal as entry point for PROV related materials, including tutorials, case studies, data sets making use of PROV, tools, forum for discussion.
  2. A special issue or article summarising the impact of provenance.
  3. An inter-operability challenge, scope of which remains to be defined, potentially involving ping-back and tool integration.

It was agreed that, in a first instance, the public-prov-comments@w3c.org mailing list should be used as a way of disseminating information and activities about PROV. To subscribe to this mailing list, follow this link.

Thanks to attendees for contributing to the workshop. More to come on the mailing lists ...
